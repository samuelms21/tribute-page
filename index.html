DR. MICHIO KAKU

Theoretical Physicist, Futurist, Bestselling Author


  • 1947 - January 24, 1947. Michio Kaku was born in San Jose, California
  • 1968 - Kaku graduated summa cum laude (with highest honor) from Harvard University 1968.
  • 1972 - Received his Ph.D. after carrying out research at University of California, Berkeley and started lecturing at Princeton University
  • 1974 - Kaku and Prof. Keiji Kikkawa from Osaka University co-authored the first papers describing string theory in a field form.
  • 1982 - 2021 - Authored and co-authored several popular science books, mostly covering string theory, space exploration and the future of the human race.
  • 2021 - Kaku currently holds the Henry Semat Chair and Professorship in theoretical physics at the City College of New York, he also published his latest book titled "The God Equation: The Quest for a Theory of Everything"

“To understand the precise point when the possible becomes the impossible, you have to appreciate and understand the laws of physics.”


- Michio Kaku, The Future of the Mind: The Scientific Quest to Understand, Enhance, and Empower the Mind
